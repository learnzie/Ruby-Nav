We would be happy to help you change the phone number on your account. Please email our Support team at accountupdates@rubynav.com with a summary of your request. To expedite the process, please send the email from the address that’s linked to your account.

How can I purchase a Ruby gift card?

Rubynav gift cards are a great way to give the gift of Anything Delivered! To purchase a Ruby gift card, go to Rubynav.com/gift-cards. Choose a design, desired amount ($25 minimum, $500 maximum), and your gift card will be sent via email. Gift cards can be applied to the entire cost of the order including fees, tip, and the cost of items. You can use Rubynav in over 100 cities across Texas.

Can I have alcohol delivered?

Rubynav delivers beer and wine from all stores. For the safety of both Rubers and customers, all deliveries are now 100% contactless. You will scan your ID and submit a selfie in the app which will be verified by your Rubers prior to delivery. If you are unresponsive, intoxicated, or unable to receive your delivery for any reason, your Rubers will return your items and you may be charged a restocking fee. In most cities, you can order beer and wine from 7am to 11pm Monday through Friday, 7am to 12am on Saturday, and 12pm to 11pm on Sunday. However, operation hours vary by market.


How does Rubynav work?

Go to Rubynav.com or download the Ruby app to browse popular nearby restaurants and Grocery stores, Errands, Gig-Pros or tap the ‘search’ icon to order from any store within your delivery zone. You can add items from our menus or type in custom orders. After you order your Rubynav, it is assigned to one of our Rubers, who will complete your delivery. Rubers act as your personal assistant that you can text throughout entire delivery, so if you decide you need to make any changes, text your Ruber to let them know. You can track your delivery and pay directly through Rubynav.com or the Ruby app. All payment information is saved securely so you only need to enter it once.


How do I pay for a Ruby?

Ruby is completely cashless. All payment is made through our app and we accept all major credit and debit cards. When you order your first Rubynav, you’ll enter your payment information, which we store securely. You only need to enter your payment info once. After you place a Rubynav, we will perform a pre-authorization on your default payment method. Additionally, you will have a short time after it’s been completed to proactively pay. During this time you can add a promo code, change the suggested Ruber tip, and choose which card you’d like to use. After this short time, we will automatically charge your default payment method and the suggested tip.
